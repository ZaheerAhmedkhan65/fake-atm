How to use:

  • Step 1: Fill in your card details in the form
  • Step 2: Click "Generate Card" to create your virtual bank card
  • Step 3: Click "Insert Card into ATM" to use the card in the ATM
  • Step 4: In the ATM, enter PIN: 1234
  • Step 5: Select transaction type and amount
  • Step 6: Choose whether to print a receipt (will download automatically)
  • Step 7: Card will be ejected after transaction completion
  • Bonus: You can download your card image for future use!
